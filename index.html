The Omakase Experience

At Omakase-ya, we honor the ancient Japanese tradition of "chef's choice" dining, where each seasonal course is carefully crafted to showcase the finest ingredients at their peak.

Nestled within the prestigious Sheraton Dubai Creek Hotel, our intimate restaurant offers an exclusive culinary journey that changes with the seasons. Our master chef sources the freshest ingredients daily, creating a unique dining experience that reflects both traditional Japanese techniques and the vibrant flavors of the season.
